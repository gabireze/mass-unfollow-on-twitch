Welcome to the Mass Unfollow on Twitch. Before getting started, please make sure you are logged in to your Twitch account and that the language is set to English.

Warning: The extension does the work for you. It starts by opening the page where your followed channels are listed. Then, it keeps scrolling down to make sure all your followed channels are shown. Once that's done, it helps you unsubscribe from those channels. All of this happens without you having to do it manually.
